FarmConnect

Developed by Aadith Tom, Alen Joe Benny, Deon Jose, and Johan Antony Davis

FarmConnect is a digital platform designed to foster a direct connection between local farmers and consumers, with a focus on reducing food waste, promoting sustainable agriculture practices, and supporting local communities. The platform serves as a marketplace where consumers can discover and purchase fresh, locally sourced produce directly from farmers, while farmers can efficiently sell their products and reduce waste by selling surplus or imperfect produce.
